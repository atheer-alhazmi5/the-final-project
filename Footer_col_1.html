OUR STORY

our journey began when both of us (Atheer & Manar) ❤ developed a love relationship with makeup & cosmetics , as arab girls, we didn't grow up seeing arab females take over the makeup industry, so we decided to make that our mission, on 10th of april 2021 our cosmetics brand "Delicate" was published, and ever since, our love for makeup and cosmetics grew even bigger. Our biggest goal when we set up Delicate Cosmetics was to make other people as passionate about makeup & cosmetics as we are. And that is still our biggest goal today.

OUR IMPACT

Supporting the women and young girls of our community, and help createing an accessible education opportunities for young women from the Middle East and helps them become strong, well-informed women that are able to speak up and change their communities and the face of the world. Delicate Cosmetics aims to reduce the misogyny against women.
